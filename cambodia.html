Cambodia

Cambodia, a Southeast Asian nation, is known for its temples, history, and culture. The Angkor Wat temple is the country’s most iconic symbol. Cambodia has a rich history that dates back thousands of years, marked by periods of significant cultural and artistic achievements. The Khmer Empire, which reached its zenith during the 9th to 15th centuries, is perhaps the most famous period in Cambodian history, known for its grand temples and impressive architectural feats.
