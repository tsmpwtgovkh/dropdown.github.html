History

Cambodia's history is characterized by the rise and fall of several powerful empires and kingdoms. The Khmer Empire, founded in the early 9th century, is the most renowned period in Cambodian history. It was known for its advanced architectural techniques and monumental construction projects, including the famous Angkor Wat temple. The decline of the Khmer Empire in the 15th century was followed by periods of colonial rule and conflict, including French colonization and the tragic Khmer Rouge regime in the 1970s. Today, Cambodia is rebuilding and preserving its cultural heritage while developing its modern identity.
